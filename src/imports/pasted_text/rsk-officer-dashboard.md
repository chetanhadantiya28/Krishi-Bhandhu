# Objective

This dashboard is the operational workspace for Rythu Seva Kendra Officers. Whenever Krishi Bandhu AI cannot confidently resolve a farmer's issue, the case is automatically escalated here. Officers can review the AI conversation, inspect uploaded images, communicate with the farmer, schedule field visits, recommend solutions or government schemes, escalate to higher authorities if necessary, and close the case. The entire experience must integrate seamlessly with the existing Krishi Bandhu ecosystem and appear as a natural extension of the current application rather than a separate product.
